Machine Learning on Breast Cancer Data

I will be predicting prognosis of breast cancer, i.e. malignant or begin tumor from biopsies.

Home Page

The incidence of breast cancer in women is the second highest behind skin cancer. The median age of diagnosis is 62. On average, 1 in 8 women will develop breast cancer in the USA. Death by breast cancer ranks second in women behind lung cancer. Further, 3.8 million patients overcome breast cancer as survivors.




The main research questions are:

  • 1. What is the most predictive metric of breast cancer?
  • 2. Which are the best ML models to predict breast cancer?
  • 3. Does the data agree with the practical standard of care for breast cancer?

Objective

To create an interactive dashboard to showcase real-world cancer data to visualize prognosis and diagnostic variables from that data. .
